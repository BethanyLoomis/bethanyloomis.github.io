About

Bloom Flower Lab is a local florist serving the Portland, OR metro area. Growing in Eagle Creek, Oregon, we specialize in professional bouquets and starts from June through October. Whatever your occasion, we’re ready to help create professional bouquets that will be the perfect centerpiece. You can find our bouquets at Quackenbush Farms or at the Hawthorne Farmers Market during the summer. Give us a call, send us an email, or message us on Instagram -- we’re excited to talk flowers with you!
